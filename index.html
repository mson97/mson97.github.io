A Little About Me...

location: seoul, korea

My name is Minhee. I am a fourth year undergraduate at the University of California, Davis studying computer science and psychology (and sometimes design)!

I'm currently working at UC Davis as a ServiceNow developer. I've had diverse experiences in programming for the campus's IT needs, teaching fellow students, and taking part in cognitive psychology research. I've always been drawn to creative problem solving and want to create products that bring humanity closer together.

I became interested in programming in my freshman year of college through an intro class in Python. I loved how everything I learned was applied immediately. Learning new things became genuinely exciting and experienced my ideas become visible reality.

Over the years, I've come to experience the joys of programming outside of just jamming away on Sublime. I enjoy the collaborative process and treasure opportunities to connect with people to build together. Though by no means a creative, I am inspired by minimal, sleek design. I am currently experimenting with design through website development, design classes, and self-practice. If you are designer or love great design, I appreciate any helpful tips or design inspiration sent my way!

In my free time, I'm researching sneaker drops, re-watching Christopher Nolan movies, or outside.
