location: seoul, korea

My name is Minhee. I am a fourth year at UC Davis studying computer science and psychology.

I'm currently working at UC Davis as a ServiceNow developer and have experience in programming, teaching, and research. I'm a natural problem solver and want to create products that bring humanity closer together in a sleek, innovative way.

Though I am not a designer, I am inspired by minimal, clean design. I am currently exploring design through web dev, design classes, and self-practice.

In my free time, I'm researching sneaker drops, re-watching Christopher Nolan movies, or jogging outside.
