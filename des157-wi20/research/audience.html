User Personas

Grace, 25

Grace is the first persona.

Grace is a 25 year old female who currently lives in New York. She recently graduated from university and is working as a designer. Much of her work is done using her computer and phone and she stays updated socially using social media. She finds that she can't really gauge her technology usage since it's so integrated into her daily life. She hopes to have a healthy relationship with technology and also appreciates sleek, interactive design.
