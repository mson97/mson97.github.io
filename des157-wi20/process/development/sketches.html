Sketches

I created a low fidelity sketches for a possible interactive website.
I am interested in the topic of technology's psychological impact.
I created three rough sketches of possible ways to explore this topic.
The first would bombard the screen with technolgy devices, notifications, etc. This webpage would convey a sense of overwhelm to the user.
The second would be like a lottery machine. The user would pull the lever and the refresh button would spin and tell the user if they have won or lost. The won or lost would be whether they have a notification.
The third would be a responsive bar chart that depicts the average technology use time in different periods from past to present.
