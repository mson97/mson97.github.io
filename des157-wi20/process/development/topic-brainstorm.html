Brainstorm

I created a mind map to help me brainstorm ideas centered around inclusiveness, respect, and reciprocity.
It helped me to expand and explore ideas related to the broader themes of connection / community, access to technology, sharing ideas / knowledge, and tech systems.
I expanded upon these subtopics using the brainstormed ideas of our own class, some online research, and my own personal experience and thoughts.
I found that the ideas of connection and community to be the most interesting as it presents the problematic psychological research findings related to heavy technology use and its addictive nature.
I also found the idea of access to technology and its various costs to our society, especially in regards to education as classrooms and schools become more reliant on technology in and outside of class time.
