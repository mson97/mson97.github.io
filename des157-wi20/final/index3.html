White Space

our personal time, this pink sliver, is incredibly important to us

As psychologist Adam Atler puts it, “That's the space where we do things that make us individuals.”

THINK ABOUT IT

In our personal time, we pursue our hobbies and passions.
We develop close relationships.
We are stimulated by the things around us and think creatively.

We pause and reflect, has our life been meaningful?

Of course, we get some significance from our other parts of life too.

But when people are in their final moments, they think about who they are and what they’ve done.
Their final words show us that the most sacred moments don’t happen at work or on a screen.
It happens in this tiny pink space: our personal time.
